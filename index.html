About the Academy

Professional music training rooted in community.

Based in Leqele High School, Maseru, the academy supports structured music education, band growth, performance training and community music initiatives.

Our website is designed to help prospective students understand available lessons, submit registration inquiries, follow events, view academy updates and connect directly with the team.
